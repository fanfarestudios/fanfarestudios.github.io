**User Accounts**

If you wish to have us delete your player account please follow these steps:
  * In app navigate to *Settings (gear icon)* -> *About*
  * Write down the displayed **Account ID**
  * Email us at *fanfare.gamestudios@gmail.com* with the Subject Line "Delete [Account ID]"
  * Important: If you open the app again a new account may be created!
